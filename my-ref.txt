Standard leave request lifecycle
-HR / Entity Admin configures leave_types (AL, ML, HL, CL …) per entity.
-On employee onboarding, system calculates statutory annual leave (staircase) and grants it on Jan 1 or anniversary.
-Each year on 1 Jan: Celery task grants statutory annual leave for all employees based on their join_date.
-Employee applies for leave (status: pending)
-Manager reviews and approves/rejects
-If approved, leave is deducted from employee's balance
-If rejected, no deduction is made 
-leave_entitlements links to employment_id. 
-A person working at 2 entities gets 2 separate leave ledgers — one per job.
